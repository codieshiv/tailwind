Our Story

A tradition of excellence since 1985

Where It All Began

Started by the Smith family in 1985, PancakeHouse began as a small breakfast spot with a passion for creating the perfect pancake. Our secret family recipe, passed down through generations, continues to delight customers to this day.

What started as a humble local diner has grown into multiple locations, but our commitment to quality and service remains unchanged.
